Red Hat Single Sign-On 7.6 Workshop

Introduction

Red Hat Single Sign-On enables you to secure your web applications by providing Web SSO capabilities based on popular standards such as OpenID Connect, OAuth 2.0, and SAML 2.0.

This is not an official guide or workshop provided by Red Hat. For official training, documentation, or guidance related to this product, please contact your designated Red Hat representative.
